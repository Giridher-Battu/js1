operators
used to perform operations betwen variables
operators are of 2 types
  • Arthematic
  • Assignment
  • relational

Arthemetic

used to perform arthematic operations

such as

  • Add(a+b)
  • sub(a-b)
  • mul(a*b)
  • div(a/b)

Assignment

used to assign the values

such as

  • x+=y => x=x+y
  • x-=y => x=x-y
  • x*=y => x=x*y
  • x/=y => x=x/y
